Low unique words has a correlation to Spotify's popularity rating.

Songs with the lowest 25% popularities tend to have the lowest average unique word count.

Country, pop and romance tend to score higher even with average word counts, while funk and punk may need to focus more on the music and less on the lyrics.

Some unique word distributions by genre breakdown.

Top 3 genres with the highest average unique word counts is Hiphop, R&B, and Classical. Although Classical has the least amout of words by genre, you get more unique words than most other categories.

Genres with the lowest average word counts are Blues, Indie-alt, and Jazz. They really are in it for the music.

If you like classical you might like jazz and metal, and if you like hiphop maybe you’ll like county too?

  • 'go' is unique to the top words in the punk and rock genres
  • 'aint' is unique to the top words in the country and hiphop genres
  • 'ooh' is unique to the top words in the indie-alt and pop genres
